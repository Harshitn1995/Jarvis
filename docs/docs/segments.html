Welcome to Jarvis's guide to getting started with User Segmentation.

What is Segmentation?

Segmentation is the practice of grouping users based on behaviors, traits, or preferences to deliver relevant messaging and drive conversions.

Thoughtful segmentation enables businesses to truly understand their audience and communicate effectively.

What is Micro-Segmentation?

Micro-segmentation is the process of breaking down broad segments into smaller, highly specific groups to ensure maximum contextual relevance and personalization in campaigns.

Why Segmentation Matters

  • Boosts Conversions: Tailored campaigns perform better.
  • Reduces Churn: Personalized engagement builds loyalty.
  • Improves ROI: Focus on high-value users and effective channels.

📌 Tip

Use segmentation strategies like RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) to refine your customer engagement at scale.

Segmentation Use-Cases

  • Drive Repeat Purchases: Recommend based on past product interest.
  • Boost App Ratings: Target happy users for reviews.
  • Increase Retention: Re-engage high-value churned users.
  • Evaluate Acquisition ROI: Segment by source to see long-term value.

Segmentation Best Practices

  • 🎯 Define Clear Goals: Know what you want each segment to do.
  • 🔍 Create Practical Micro-Segments: Smaller, focused groups work better.
  • 🛠️ Segment by Lifecycle Stage: Address users at each journey stage.
  • 📏 Keep Segments Actionable: Avoid over-segmentation.
