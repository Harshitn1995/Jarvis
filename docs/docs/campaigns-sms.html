Welcome to Jarvis's guide to getting started with SMS Marketing

Let's Get You Started

Engage users offline with personalized and actionable messages to create seamless brand experiences. Backed by real-time insights and intuitive features, you are bound to deliver high-impact messages each time!

Step 1: Integrate your Jarvis account with an SSP (SMS Service Provider) of your choice.

Step 2: Segment your users by their preferences or behavior to engage them with contextual messages.

Step 3: Create an SMS campaign through the Channel or a Journey.

Step 4: Analyze the SMS campaign against various performance indicators to understand its impact on user engagement, conversions, and revenue.

Step 5: Analyze the overall impact of SMS as a Channel to understand its effectiveness in helping you meet your business goals.

Step 6: Have these insights emailed periodically to your team by setting up scheduled reports for an SMS campaign and all campaigns sent through the channel.

And, if you're new to the world of marketing automation, or find yourself lost in a world of technical jargon, then head over to Key Concepts of User Engagement for quick answers!
