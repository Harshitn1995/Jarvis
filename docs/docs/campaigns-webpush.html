Welcome to Jarvis's guide to getting started with WebPush Marketing

Let's Get You Started

Engage off-site users across web and mobile web with actionable web push notifications. Backed by user insights and powerful features, you are bound to create high-impact experiences each time!

Step 1: Configure Web Push opt-in for your website.

Step 2: Segment your users by their preferences and behavior to engage them with contextual messages.

Step 3: Create a Web Push campaign through the Channel or a Journey.

Step 4: Analyze the Web Push campaign against various performance indicators to understand its impact on user engagement, conversions, and revenue.

Step 5: Analyze the overall impact of Web Push as a Channel to understand its effectiveness in helping you meet your business goals.

Step 6: Have these insights emailed periodically to your team by setting up scheduled reports for a Web Push campaign and all campaigns sent through the channel.

And if you're new to the world of marketing automation or feel lost in technical jargon, check out the Key Concepts of User Engagement for quick answers!
